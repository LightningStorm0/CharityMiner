Charity Miner

How does this work?

This website works by mining crypto-currency a digital form of money. "What is crypto-currency mining", you may ask? Crypto-currency mining is a way that crypto-currency companies distribute their currency. Crypto-currency is mined by clients computers verifying transactions so the company doesn't have to. Hashes are hard for computers to do, so the companies include them and make them easier or harder to keep the supply of un-verified transactions at a steady number. This website mines crypto-currency in your browser that then get's donated to charity!
